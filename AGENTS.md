# Project continuity instructions

Before planning or changing this repository, read `PROJECT_CONTEXT.md` completely.

After completing a material feature, decision, deployment change, or workflow change:

1. Update `PROJECT_CONTEXT.md` with the durable outcome.
2. Record decisions and current state, not a verbatim chat transcript.
3. Never store access tokens, passwords, customer secrets, financial account details, or other credentials.
4. Keep historical facts concise and mark superseded decisions rather than silently deleting important context.
5. Verify the application before updating the “Current verified state” section.
